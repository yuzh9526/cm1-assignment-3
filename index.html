Here is my Favourtie Cake! Japanese cheesecake is my favorite cake. It is not difficult to make but requires great care. You need to be patient and careful when you make it and cook it

Ingredient

Egg, Butter, Cake Flour, Sugar, Cream Cheess and Corn Starch

The taste

This cake is not as sweet as cheesecake and tastes better. It's a little bit like a sponge but it also has a cheesecake texture. The taste of this cake is also what I like about it. Put it in the fridge for a while and it will taste better！

My handmake Pumpkin Lamp

This is my lantern

I suddenly wanted to do something on Halloween, so I chose the jack-o '-lantern. After finishing, I put it on the balcony, very interesting and good-looking.

Processing

  1. Buy a pumpkin in the market
  2. Clean it. Cut off the top of the pumpkin and hollow out the inside.
  3. Draw your favorite shape on the paper
  4. Put the paper on the pumpkin
  5. According to the shape, cut the pumpkin.
  6. Put a candle in the pumpkin and finish
